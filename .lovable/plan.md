

# ArthaSakhi — Financial Literacy Community Website

A clean, elegant one-page static website for a women's financial literacy initiative with earthy, warm design aesthetics.

## Design Theme
- **Colors**: Warm beige, soft brown, muted green, off-white — calm and trustworthy
- **Typography**: Clean serif headings + readable sans-serif body text
- **Feel**: Grassroots, community-driven, authentic, professional

## Sections (in scroll order)

### 1. Hero Section
Large title "ArthaSakhi" with subtitle and tagline about empowering women with financial knowledge. Warm background with subtle SVG illustrations of community and growth.

### 2. About the Community
Story of how ArthaSakhi grew from offline gatherings into a vibrant community movement focused on financial confidence for women and families.

### 3. Our Mission
Centered, impactful mission statement about promoting financial literacy and responsible investing for women and families.

### 4. What We Focused On
Grid/list of focus areas — financial planning, savings awareness, mutual funds, goal-based investing, etc. Clean icons alongside each point.

### 5. Community Impact
Storytelling section about how women gained confidence in financial discussions. Key impact highlights with warm visual treatment.

### 6. Impact Snapshot (Statistics Grid)
Clean grid with 4 metric cards, each with an icon, large animated number (count-up on scroll), and short description:
- Community Sessions Conducted
- Women Participants Engaged
- Families Reached
- Years of Activity

Numbers easily editable. Subtle earthy color accents.

### 7. Community Moments (Visual Storytelling)
Image gallery with soft rounded frames, earthy backgrounds, subtle shadows, and hover effects. Placeholder images with captions like "Financial awareness session with community women." Easy to swap with real photos.

### 8. Why Financial Awareness Matters
Centered section highlighting security, independence, informed decisions, and building stable futures.

### 9. Founder Spotlight (Two-Column)
- **Left**: Portrait placeholder with decorative frame
- **Right**: Bio of Ms Hema Poonia + highlighted quote block with elegant quote marks and warm accent colors. Subtle background pattern.

### 10. A Growing Movement (Closing)
Full-width section with soft background, centered text about the growing awareness movement. Subtle growth/community visual element. Ends with the strong empowerment statement.

### 11. Footer
Founder credit, title, and tagline. Clean and minimal.

## Interactions & Polish
- Smooth scroll navigation
- Fade-in animations on scroll (Intersection Observer)
- Count-up animation for impact numbers
- Subtle hover effects on cards and images
- Mobile responsive throughout
- SVG decorative elements (leaves, coins, growth motifs) as section accents
